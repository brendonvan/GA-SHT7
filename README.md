# <img src='./public/assets/Chore_coin.svg' > Chores for Coins

## Winner of General Assembly's 2023 Spring Hack-A-Thon

The app was designed to assist parents in teaching their children the value of money and patience. Our app rewards kids with coins for completing tasks or chores while working toward a goal or price agreed upon by their parents!
